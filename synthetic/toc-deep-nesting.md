# TOC deep nesting

This page exists to stress the right-hand "On this page" TOC, which is the
subject of [issue #387](https://github.com/QuantEcon/quantecon-book-theme/issues/387)
(TOC overflow and sidebar crowding). It contains many headings at varying
depths so that:

- The RHS TOC is long enough to overflow the viewport vertically.
- Nested headings exercise the indent-per-level rendering at depths 2–6.
- The LHS sidebar treatment under a densely-headed page is visible.

If the theme regresses, this page will either truncate, scroll incorrectly,
or look visually crowded — all of which should jump out in the snapshot diff.
